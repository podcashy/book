Delivering the Message of Healing and Restoration…

Since the moment God heard my cry from the depths of the sea and came to my rescue, I have been on a mission to share my experience, to empower, teach, and rescue others who may find themselves in the grip of despair, frustration, or hopelessness. I pen down these words with the hope that they may find their way to someone who is currently navigating life's turbulent waters.

"For I know the plans I have for you,” declares the LORD, “plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future.” — Jeremiah 29:11

The conditions set by God are simple yet pivotal: Firstly, acknowledge that suffering often arises from our own sins (Isaiah 59:1-2). Secondly, turn to Him wholeheartedly (Zechariah 1:3). Finally, return to the crossroads of your life and choose the path of righteousness (Jeremiah 6:16). When you do, earnestly seeking Him with all your heart, God will extend His mighty hand.

"I will go before thee, and make the rough places smooth; I will break in pieces the doors of brass, and cut in sunder the bars of iron; and I will give thee the treasures of darkness, and hidden riches of secret places..." — Isaiah 45:2-3

I stand as a living testament to God's power to humble and use anyone. Everything I am, everything I have, and everywhere I find myself today is a testament to His grace. You will encounter the God of restoration, the One who will restore everything that the devil has stolen from you.

Remember the Shunnamite woman and how God restored her son, her land, her house, and her produce through Elisha (2 Kings 8:6). God's power to restore knows no bounds.

Use this incredible key with wisdom and discernment. Let the upcoming days and the work you engage in tell a story of human triumph—one you will never be ashamed of, and one that future generations of your family will proudly recount.

There Is Nothing To Gain From Drug And Substance Abuse

If engaging in smoking, drinking, chewing, or any form of substance abuse were a lucrative investment, I would likely be a millionaire by now. However, the truth is that I reaped nothing but loss from my years of disobedience and indulgence.

I lost a full 12 years of my life, valuable sponsorships, my health, and countless other losses that I'd rather not dwell on. I'm well aware of many individuals who have lost jobs, families, contracted severe illnesses, or are living deeply distressing lives due to their involvement in drug abuse.

I've never come across a manager who received a promotion for being an exceptional drinker. On the contrary, I can introduce you to numerous people who were once affluent but became impoverished and bankrupt because they fell into the trap of alcoholism.

Take Kimani, for instance. He was once a permanent employee at Coca Cola Company earning a generous salary. Today, he resides in a ramshackle dwelling in Vietnam village, where his closest neighbors are pigs. I've personally known individuals who sold everything they owned—household items, phones, clothes—just to afford beer and other drugs.

"Thus says the Lord, your Redeemer, the Holy One of Israel: 'I am the Lord your God, who teaches you to profit, who leads you in the way you should go.'" — Isaiah 48:17

Stay sober, avoid drugs, and spare yourself the painful losses I endured during those wasted 12 years of my life!

Better Stay Alone and Be Safe!

In my ongoing mission to reach out to young people, I've had the privilege of giving talks in numerous public and private schools. I consistently emphasize the importance of choosing friends with great care.

"Walk with the wise and become wise, for a companion of fools suffers harm." — Proverbs 13:20

I ardently advocate for the idea that it's far better to stand alone with your principles intact than to surround yourself with many who might lead you astray. Forge friendships within the church community and among God-fearing people. These connections serve as pillars of strength.

I implore all churches to embrace and expand programs like youth camps. These platforms offer a haven for personal growth, self-discovery, and the development of a strong moral compass. Through such efforts, we empower young individuals to navigate life's challenges with resilience and grace.
