Foreword

"Rise and Fall: Battleground of Blessings and Envy"

In the grand theatre of life, every individual has the potential to ascend to great heights or tumble into the abyss. As the Almighty bestows His blessings upon His people, the malevolent forces of envy and resentment lurk in the shadows. Day and night, the devil toils tirelessly, relentlessly opposing God's creations. Without a steadfast foundation rooted in the divine, one stands vulnerable to the sinister onslaught and the inevitable plunge into darkness.

Whenever the benevolent hand of God reaches out to bless us, He often employs a human vessel. Conversely, when malevolence seeks to strike, it too employs a human agent, frequently disguising itself as a friend or even a close relative. The adversary is rarely a distant entity; more often than not, it is someone intimately familiar, a companion who walks alongside us.

The narrative you are about to embark upon is no work of fiction; it is an unvarnished account of a life's journey. God paved the way, granting excellence in academic pursuits and a full scholarship to a prestigious national school. Excellence continued through the KCSE of 2002, leading to admission at JKUAT to pursue Computer Science. God's blessings were abundant, including a benevolent German sponsor who supported the entire family's education.

However, the story takes a sudden twist. A grave error in judgment during college years led to an entanglement with vices — smoking, drinking, and chewing miraa. The devil ensnared his life in the web of addiction for a harrowing twelve-year ordeal.

Why share this painful narrative? The answer lies in the encounter with countless intelligent, gifted young individuals currently trapped in the quagmire of substance abuse. Upon a miraculous transformation, the author made a solemn commitment to educate the community. A graduate of the "University of Hard Knocks," he has harnessed his own experiences to catalyze change among fellow addicts.

This book is a must-read for every child. It is a tool for equipping themselves with invaluable knowledge. The author has dedicated his life to mentoring, motivating, and inspiring young people to unearth their potential. He extends an earnest call to those willing to partner with him in this noble cause.

To God be the glory!

F. Mureithi
Lecturer & Lead Counselor, PUC
