The Resurrection of a Dream

Despite the twelve harrowing years I spent in the wilderness of addiction, I never truly let go of the spark God placed in my heart. When I finally emerged from the "House of Doom," I attempted to return to campus to complete my degree in Computer Science. However, life presented yet another wall: I could not afford to raise the fees required to finish my formal education.

To many, this would have been the final defeat. But I had already learned the power of the U-Turn. I resolved that if the doors of the lecture halls were closed, the doors of the digital world remained wide open. I turned to online learning with a hunger born from years of lost time.

"From Ashes to Code"

Through discipline and divine favor, I mastered the art of programming. Today, I am the lead developer of the Masomo Digital Library—an ecosystem of applications designed to empower our community in business, communication, and financial record-keeping.

Explore Masomo Apps

My journey from a university dropout and an addict to a software developer is not a testament to my own strength, but to the reality of possibility. I have written this testimony to remind every youth, every parent, and every struggling soul that your past does not have the final say.

Change is a possibility as long as you are willing to take that U-turn. Never give up on your dreams, for the same God who rescued me from the pit has empowered me to build tools that serve thousands today.

"It is never too late to become what you might have been."
