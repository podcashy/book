The Bus Analogy Reimagined

We've established that life is a journey filled with numerous challenges. The reason why many individuals fall and never rise again is often due to denial—they refuse to acknowledge that they've taken a path leading away from their intended destination.

The Wrong Bus: Picture arriving at the bus stop, but you mistakenly board a bus headed in the wrong direction. What should you do the moment you realize you're on the wrong bus? You should promptly raise your voice, signal the driver to halt, and disembark immediately! Make a U-turn, return to the point where you deviated, and board the correct route to your destination.

The Hijacked Bus Analogy

Now, consider a scenario where you've boarded a bus headed for your desired destination. Suddenly, the bus is hijacked by criminals. They dictate where the bus goes, holding you hostage. Your only hope lies in external assistance—the police need to intervene and rescue you.

However, when you are spiritually hijacked by the enemy, you don't require the police! What you need is a higher, spiritual authority: God Himself. God will only intervene when you open the door to your heart, allowing Him to take control. It is your committed decision to turn to Him that will ultimately set you free!

Parenting & Technology

In the endeavor to guide our children to realize their full potential, technology has become a significant and indispensable ally. Technology represents innovation and is a force for good when employed specifically for the purpose of parenting.

Merits of Technology for Today’s Parent:

  • Communication: Through cell phones, we can know the whereabouts of our children while we are at work.
  • Monitoring: Digital systems can monitor if children have reported to school safely.
  • Provision: Many parents work online (e.g., selling books on Amazon) to provide for their families.
  • Education: Teachers can use digital platforms designed specifically for learning.
  • Spirituality: Bible apps allow constant access to the Word without the need for heavy hardcovers.
  • Efficiency: Making transactions and paying bills through M-Pesa allows for more quality family time.
The Challenge:

On the other hand, technological advancement has come up to demoralize our society and make the work of parenting a nightmare for many.

Negative Effects of Misusing Technology & Social Media:

1. Erosion of Moral Values

Exposure to explicit and inappropriate content can desensitize youths to morally objectionable behavior, leading them away from the path of righteousness.

2. Cyber-Bullying and Mental Health

Social media can be a breeding ground for harassment. Constant comparison with the "filtered" lives of others often leads to low self-esteem, anxiety, and depression.

3. Addiction and Wasted Time

The "infinite scroll" of apps is designed to keep youths hooked. This leads to the neglect of studies, physical exercise, and real-life social interactions.

4. Vulnerability to Predators

Without proper supervision, the internet gives strangers access to children, putting them at risk of exploitation and harmful radicalization.

5. Sleep Deprivation

Late-night browsing disrupts the natural sleep cycle, affecting brain development, concentration in school, and overall physical health.
